About Us

 
           Who Are We?

 

Launched in Mumbai 2 years ago, Vibe-Eats has grown from a home project to some of the largest food aggregators in India We are present in 14 States and 1000+ cities, enabling our vision of better food for more people. We not only connect people to food in every context but work closely with restaurants to enable a sustainable ecosystem.

           Our Journey

 

Back in 2008, all it took was an idea to enable digital access to thousands of restaurant menus. Three passionate foodies who hated waiting in lines, drove around Mumbai to collect menus from restaurants, scan them and put them online. Their idea has now grown into the vision that drives our team of 5000+ people everyday.

 
             Our Clients
 
McDonald's

 
McDonald's Corporation is an American fast food company, founded in 1940 as a restaurant operated by Richard and Maurice McDonald, in San Bernardino, California, United States. They rechristened their business as a hamburger stand, and later turned the company into a franchise, with the Golden Arches logo being introduced in 1953 at a location in Phoenix, Arizona. In 1955, Ray Kroc, a businessman, joined the company as a franchise agent and proceeded to purchase the chain from the McDonald brothers. McDonald's had its original headquarters in Oak Brook, Illinois, but moved its global headquarters to Chicago in June 2018.
 
Pizza Hut

 

 
Pizza Hut is an American restaurant chain and international franchise which was founded in 1958 in Wichita, Kansas by Dan and Frank Carney. The company is known for its Italian American cuisine menu, including pizza and pasta, as well as side dishes and desserts. Pizza Hut has 18,703 restaurants worldwide as of December 31, 2019,making it the world's largest pizza chain in terms of locations. It is a subsidiary of Yum! Brands, Inc., one of the world's largest restaurant companies.
 
Lake View Cafe

 

 
This all-day dining place in its bright new avatar, with an al fresco section, is a treat for the eyes and a far cry from the staid place it was. The décor is smart and contemporary and the vibe, unmistakably warm and inviting. The layout is pleasing and the seating areas well-spread out. From 'Nasik Tomato Soup' to 'Saoji Mutton' and 'Prawns Linguine' to 'Brie Tempura', the exhaustive a la carte menu entices you with myriad offerings across cuisines. There are classics, Indian dishes, small plates, large plates and desserts. The comfort food - 'Chicken Baida Roll', 'Mumbai Toastie' and 'Matke Chole with Achari Kulche', certainly merit a try. The buffet spread for lunch and dinner is equally an exercise in choices. The selection on the bar menu is appealing, with quirky and fun offerings too. No drama or gimmicks, in the well-presented food here, but the dishes scream quality in terms of ingredients and bold flavours. The desserts section offers a range of global deserts and Indian sweets. The 'Kerala Coffee Cardamom Crème Brulee' is exceptional. The staff is affable, articulate and the service warm and alert
 
Oheka

 

 
This expansive sky deck and bar with elegant décor, is spread over multiple seating areas - including an outdoor pool-facing section and an indoor area. The pool-facing area is the most coveted place to be seated. The food menu is fairly limited and offers North Indian and predictable global fare. The bar menu is exhaustive and offers sufficient variety of craft cocktails. For mains, the 'Butter Chicken' paired with 'Tandoori Roti' is a safe bet, as there are not too many options. For a quick bite, there is pizza and pasta. The food is well-presented, the portion sizes are generous, but the flavours are steeped in mediocrity. The service staff is extremely polite and efficient. This place is a hotspot for late-night party revellers.
Candies

 
What was once a tiny take away shack has now morphed into three outlets in Bandra. The idea here is quick service and fast food, so you pick from the array of dishes on display, pay for your food at the counter and then find a spot to enjoy it. Usually full of college kids hanging out and grabbing a snack but the lines can get serpent like during lunch hours. The Puffs, Patty and Rolls are all a rage and fly off the counter but their wide choice of desserts are just as popular.
